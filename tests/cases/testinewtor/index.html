Basic Form Filling with Ghostlab

Enter something in a form field below. You'll see that the field will be filled out in sync on the other browsers.

Remove Form Filling

Triple-Click on any form field to open the remote form filling tool. It will show you inputs for the field in every browser.

Automatic Form Filling

Place the cursor in a form field. Then use the Automatic Form Filling shortcut (Ctrl-Shift-F by default) to fill out this entire form below!

Quickfills

Use Quickfills to quickly populate a field: e.g., type [code $e], which will expand to a random e-mail address when you leave the form field.

Round-Robin Data

Define datasets for quickfills to enter different values on every client. E.g. $0
